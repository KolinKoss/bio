Kevin Gates

Kevin Jerome Gilyard (better known by the stage name Kevin Gates) is an American rapper, singer, and entrepreneur. He is signed to Bread Winner's Association with a partnership with Atlantic Records. His debut studio album, Islah, released in January 2016 and rose to number 2 on the US Billboard 200 chart. Prior to his debut he released numerous popular mixtapes. He is now one of the most famous rappers ever.

Gates has 1 studio album, 21 singles, 2 EPs, and 16 mixtapes. He has released all of these over the course of about 12 years while being in and out of jail.
